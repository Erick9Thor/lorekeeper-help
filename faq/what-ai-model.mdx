## Smart AI, No Configuration Needed

LOREKEEPER uses a multi-provider approach to deliver the best AI Dungeon Master experience. You never need to choose or configure an AI model -- the platform handles it automatically.

## How It Works

Instead of locking into a single AI provider, LOREKEEPER selects the best available model based on your plan tier. This means you always get optimal quality for your subscription level without any manual setup.

## AI Quality by Plan

### Free Tier

Free accounts receive a quality AI model that delivers engaging, coherent storytelling. When you first sign up, you get a **3-day trial at Legend-tier quality** so you can experience the full power of the AI DM before committing to a plan.

### Paid Tiers

Paid plans guarantee access to **premium AI models** that provide richer narratives, better memory of past events, more nuanced NPC interactions, and deeper tactical awareness during combat.

<Tip>Start with the free trial to experience Legend-quality AI. You will immediately notice the depth and creativity of the Dungeon Master's storytelling.</Tip>

## Continuous Improvement

As AI technology advances, LOREKEEPER upgrades the models available to each tier. These improvements happen automatically -- you benefit from better storytelling without needing to update anything or change any settings.

## Privacy and AI

Your game data is used exclusively to run your sessions. It is never fed back into AI model training. See our data privacy article for full details.

<Note>The AI DM's quality depends on more than just the model. LOREKEEPER's custom prompt engineering, context management, and world-aware systems work together to create a Dungeon Master that understands your story, your characters, and your world.</Note>
