## Play Anywhere, No App Store Needed

LOREKEEPER is a **Progressive Web App (PWA)**, which means it runs directly in your browser with an experience that feels like a native app. No downloads from an app store required.

## Supported Browsers

LOREKEEPER works on any modern mobile browser:

- **Chrome** (Android and iOS)
- **Safari** (iOS and macOS)
- **Firefox** (Android)
- **Edge** (Android and iOS)

The interface is optimized for screens as small as **375px wide**, which covers virtually all modern smartphones.

## Installing on Your Home Screen

For the best mobile experience, install LOREKEEPER as an app on your device:

### Android (Chrome)

1. Open LOREKEEPER in Chrome.
2. Tap the three-dot menu in the top right.
3. Select **Add to Home Screen** or **Install App**.
4. LOREKEEPER appears on your home screen like a regular app.

### iOS (Safari)

1. Open LOREKEEPER in Safari.
2. Tap the **Share** button (square with upward arrow).
3. Scroll down and tap **Add to Home Screen**.
4. LOREKEEPER appears on your home screen with its own icon.

<Tip>Installing as a PWA gives you a full-screen experience without browser bars, faster load times, and easy access from your home screen.</Tip>

## Full Functionality

The mobile version includes everything the desktop version offers:

- Campaign management and gameplay
- Character creation and editing
- World building and community browsing
- Multiplayer with friends
- Combat and exploration

<Note>For extended sessions, keep your device charged. Complex AI-generated narratives and combat encounters work best on a stable connection.</Note>
