## Your Data, Your Story

LOREKEEPER takes data privacy seriously. Your campaigns, characters, and personal information are protected by multiple layers of security and strict privacy policies.

## Where Data Is Stored

All game data and account information is stored on **European servers**. This means your data benefits from the strong privacy protections of European data regulations.

## Not Used for AI Training

Your game sessions, character details, world content, and conversations with the AI Dungeon Master are **never used to train AI models**. Your creative content belongs to you, not to an AI training pipeline.

<Tip>You can play and create freely knowing that your stories, worlds, and characters remain private and are not repurposed for any external use.</Tip>

## GDPR Compliance

LOREKEEPER is fully compliant with the **General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)**. This means:

- You have the right to access all data we store about you.
- You have the right to correct inaccurate data.
- You have the right to request deletion of your data.
- We only collect data necessary to provide the service.

## Account Deletion

If you decide to leave LOREKEEPER, you can request full account deletion from your account settings. This permanently removes your account, campaigns, characters, and all associated data from our servers.

<Note>Account deletion is irreversible. Make sure to export or save any content you want to keep before requesting deletion.</Note>

## Security Practices

- Encrypted connections (HTTPS) for all data in transit.
- Secure authentication with industry-standard protocols.
- Regular security audits and updates.
- Access controls that ensure only you can see your private content.
