## Play in Your Language

LOREKEEPER supports four languages across the entire platform, from the interface to the AI Dungeon Master's narration.

## Interface Languages

The full application interface is available in:

- **Spanish** (Espanol)
- **English**
- **Catalan** (Catala)
- **Portuguese** (Portugues)

Every button, menu, tooltip, and system message is translated. You can switch languages at any time.

## How to Change Your Language

There are two ways to change your language:

1. **Header selector** -- Click the language icon in the top navigation bar and select your preferred language.
2. **Settings** -- Go to your account settings and update your language preference.

The change takes effect immediately across the entire application.

<Tip>Your language preference is saved to your account, so it persists across devices and sessions.</Tip>

## AI Dungeon Master Language

The AI DM responds in your selected language. Whether you are playing in Spanish, English, Catalan, or Portuguese, the narration, NPC dialogue, and combat descriptions are all generated in the language you choose.

## World Content Translation

If you build a custom world, you can translate its content to make it available in all four languages:

- Translation covers races, classes, items, spells, locations, and monsters.
- Each entity costs **1 credit** to translate.
- Translations are AI-generated and can be manually reviewed and edited.
- Translated worlds reach a wider audience in the Community Worlds browser.

<Note>World content translation is a paid feature available to world creators. It is separate from the interface language, which is free for all users.</Note>
