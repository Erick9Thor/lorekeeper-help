## What Are Credits?

Credits are LOREKEEPER's flexible currency. While daily turns handle your core play sessions, credits let you unlock extras -- additional rounds, AI-generated images, new characters, campaigns, and more.

## Welcome Credits

Every new account receives **10 welcome credits** on signup. These are yours to spend however you like, no strings attached.

## Monthly Credits from Plans

Paid plans include a monthly credit allowance that refreshes each billing cycle:

| Plan | Monthly Credits |
|------|----------------|
| Free | -- |
| Aventurero | 25 |
| Heroe | 60 |
| Leyenda | 150 |

## Credit Packs

Need more credits? You can purchase packs at any time from the Store page:

| Pack | Credits | Price |
|------|---------|-------|
| Small | 15 | 2.99 EUR |
| Medium | 40 | 5.99 EUR |
| Chest | 100 | 12.99 EUR |

<Tip>The Chest pack offers the best value per credit. If you find yourself buying credits regularly, it is the most efficient option.</Tip>

## What Can You Spend Credits On?

- **Extra campaign rounds** -- 1 credit converts to 3 rounds. Useful when you run out of daily turns and want to keep playing.
- **AI-generated images** -- Character portraits, scene illustrations, and item art.
- **Additional characters** -- Create more heroes beyond your plan limit.
- **Additional campaigns** -- Open new worlds and stories.
- **Custom worlds** -- Build your own settings for you and your friends.

## Earning Free Credits

You do not always have to pay. There are two ways to earn credits for free:

- **Referral program:** Invite a friend using your referral code. When they sign up, you both receive **5 credits**.
- **Discord bonus:** Join the LOREKEEPER Discord server and link your account for **5 extra credits**.

<Note>Credits purchased through packs never expire. Use them whenever you are ready.</Note>
