## Which Plan Is Right for You?

**Free** is perfect for trying LOREKEEPER out. You get 20 turns per day -- enough for a solid session -- and one campaign to explore.

**Aventurero** (4.99 EUR/mo) is ideal for regular players who want more depth. Campaign memory means the AI DM remembers your story across sessions, and 25 monthly credits give you room for images and extra rounds.

**Heroe** (9.99 EUR/mo) suits players who run multiple campaigns or characters. With 60 daily turns and 5 campaign slots, you can explore different worlds without constraint.

**Leyenda** (19.99 EUR/mo) is for power users and game enthusiasts. Unlimited turns, unlimited campaigns, maximum AI quality, and 150 monthly credits. No limits.

<Tip>Every new account starts with a 3-day trial at Leyenda quality. Use those days to experience the full platform before choosing a plan.</Tip>

<Note>All paid plans can be cancelled anytime. You keep your features until the end of the billing period.</Note>
