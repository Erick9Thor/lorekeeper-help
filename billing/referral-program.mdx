## Share the Adventure, Earn Credits

LOREKEEPER's referral program rewards you for bringing new players to the platform. When someone signs up using your referral code, **both of you receive 5 free credits**.

## How It Works

1. **Find your referral code.** Go to your **Profile** page or the **Invite** section. Your unique referral link and code are displayed there.
2. **Share it.** Send the link to a friend through any channel -- chat, email, social media, or carrier pigeon.
3. **They sign up.** When your friend creates a LOREKEEPER account using your referral link, the system registers the connection.
4. **Both of you earn.** Once signup is complete, 5 credits are added to your account and 5 credits to theirs. Instantly.

## What Can You Do with 5 Credits?

Five credits go further than you might think:

- Convert them to **15 extra campaign rounds** (1 credit = 3 rounds).
- Use them for an **AI-generated character portrait**.
- Save them up across multiple referrals for bigger purchases.

<Tip>There is no cap on how many people you can refer. Every successful referral earns you 5 credits. Invite your whole game group and stock up.</Tip>

## Where to Find Your Referral Link

- **Profile page** -- Your referral code and shareable link are shown in your account settings.
- **Invite Hub** -- A dedicated section with sharing options for social media and direct links.

## Important Details

- The referred person must be a **new account**. Existing accounts do not qualify.
- Credits are awarded **immediately** after the new user completes registration.
- Referral credits never expire.

<Note>Combine referral credits with the Discord bonus (5 credits for joining the server) to maximize your free credit balance from day one.</Note>
