## A Tabletop RPG, Reimagined

LOREKEEPER is an AI-powered virtual tabletop RPG platform. You create a character, pick a world, and dive into a story narrated by an intelligent AI Game Master -- always available, always ready to play.

No scheduling conflicts. No waiting for your group to be free on a Thursday night. Just open the app and start your adventure.

## How It Works

The AI DM (Dungeon Master) drives the narrative. It describes scenes, controls non-player characters, resolves combat, tracks your inventory, and remembers what happened in previous sessions. You interact through natural language -- type what your character does, and the world responds.

Think of it as a living, breathing story that adapts to your choices in real time.

## What You Can Do

- **Create campaigns** across 11 world genres, from classic fantasy to cyberpunk and post-apocalyptic settings.
- **Build characters** with distinct races, classes, spells, and equipment.
- **Explore and fight** through tactical combat with initiative, conditions, damage types, and karmic dice.
- **Play with friends** in multiplayer sessions with up to 6 players, each with a defined role like Leader, Scout, or Healer.
- **Play solo** whenever you want -- the AI DM is always on.

## How It Differs from Traditional TTRPGs

In a traditional tabletop RPG, you need a human Game Master and a group of players who can all meet at the same time. LOREKEEPER removes that barrier. The AI DM is available 24/7, sessions last as long as you want, and you can pause and resume at any time.

<Tip>New to tabletop RPGs? LOREKEEPER is a great place to start. The AI DM will guide you through everything -- no rulebook required.</Tip>
