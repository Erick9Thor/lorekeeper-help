## Your Personal Dungeon Master

LOREKEEPER's AI Dungeon Master is the engine behind every adventure. It processes your actions in real time, weaving narrative descriptions and mechanical resolutions into a seamless experience. When you swing a sword, the DM rolls dice, calculates damage, and describes the result in a single response.

## How It Processes Actions

Every time you type an action, the AI DM handles two layers simultaneously:

- **Narrative layer**: Describes what happens in the story with vivid, genre-appropriate prose.
- **Mechanical layer**: Resolves dice rolls, damage calculations, skill checks, and status effects behind the scenes.

You never have to worry about rules. The DM applies them for you and explains outcomes clearly.

## Memory and Context

The AI DM remembers your adventure. It tracks message history, key facts about your character, campaign events, and the state of the world. When you mention an NPC you met three sessions ago, the DM knows who you are talking about.

<Tip>Higher subscription tiers provide more context memory, which means the DM can recall more details and produce richer narrative.</Tip>

## What the DM Manages Automatically

- **NPCs**: Creates and controls non-player characters with distinct personalities.
- **Quests**: Generates objectives dynamically based on your story choices.
- **Combat**: Handles initiative, damage, conditions, and loot distribution.
- **World state**: Tracks locations, events, and consequences of your decisions.

## Narrative Arcs

The DM follows a 7-phase pacing structure: hook, rising action, midpoint, complications, climax, falling action, and resolution. This ensures your adventure has satisfying dramatic progression rather than a random sequence of events.

<Note>The AI DM supports 11 world genres, from high fantasy to cyberpunk, and adapts its vocabulary, tone, and mechanics to match.</Note>
