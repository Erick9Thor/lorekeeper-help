## Never Lose Your Place

Life gets busy. You might step away from your adventure for hours, days, or even weeks. LOREKEEPER's session recap feature ensures you can always pick up exactly where you left off without confusion.

## How It Works

When you return to a campaign after a gap of 30 minutes or more, the AI DM automatically detects the break. Before continuing the story, it generates a concise recap of what happened in your previous session.

The recap covers:

- **Key events**: Major story beats, combat outcomes, and important discoveries.
- **NPC interactions**: Who you spoke to and what was discussed.
- **Quest progress**: Updates on your active objectives.
- **Current situation**: Where your character is and what they were doing when you left.

<Tip>Read the recap carefully. It often reminds you of details and clues that are easy to forget between sessions.</Tip>

## Seamless Continuation

After the recap, the DM transitions smoothly back into the narrative. There is no awkward restart or need to re-explain your intentions. You are back in the story as if you never left.

## Designed for Real Life

Not everyone can play for hours in a single sitting. Session recap is built for the reality of modern life: play for 20 minutes on your lunch break, come back in the evening, and the DM bridges the gap for you.

<Note>Session recaps are injected into the AI's context automatically. The DM does not just summarize for you, it also refreshes its own memory of your adventure to maintain narrative consistency.</Note>
