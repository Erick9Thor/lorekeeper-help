## Make It Yours

One of LOREKEEPER's most powerful features is the ability to create fully custom content for your worlds. Every race, class, item, spell, and monster you design becomes part of the living world your AI Dungeon Master uses to tell stories.

## Custom Races

Define new species with their own identity:

- **Name and description** -- Who they are and where they come from.
- **Attributes** -- Stat modifiers that affect gameplay.
- **Lore** -- Cultural background, history, and traits that the AI DM weaves into the narrative.

## Custom Classes

Build character archetypes from scratch:

- **Name and description** -- The class identity and fantasy.
- **Abilities** -- Unique skills and powers available to characters of this class.
- **Progression** -- How the class evolves as characters grow.

## Custom Items and Spells

Populate your world with unique gear and magic:

- **Items** -- Weapons, armor, consumables, quest objects, and artifacts with custom stats and effects.
- **Spells** -- Magical abilities with descriptions, costs, and mechanical effects.

<Tip>The AI DM reads your item and spell descriptions to determine how they work in the narrative. Detailed descriptions lead to richer storytelling.</Tip>

## Custom Monsters

Design creatures and enemies for your world:

- **Stats and abilities** -- Define how tough they are and what they can do.
- **Behavior and lore** -- The AI DM uses this to roleplay monsters with personality and tactics.

## How Custom Content Works with the AI DM

Everything you create is fed directly into the AI Dungeon Master's context. When a player encounters your custom race, uses your custom spell, or fights your custom monster, the AI DM references your descriptions and stats to generate accurate, immersive responses.

<Note>Well-written descriptions give the AI DM better material to work with. A few sentences of lore go a long way toward making encounters memorable.</Note>
