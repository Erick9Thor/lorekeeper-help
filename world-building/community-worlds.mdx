## A Universe Built by Players

LOREKEEPER's community worlds let you explore settings created by other players. From dark cyberpunk cities to sprawling fantasy kingdoms, there is always a new world waiting for your next campaign.

## Browsing Worlds

Visit the **Community Worlds** section from the main dashboard to see published worlds. You can filter by genre, language, and rating to find exactly what you are looking for. Each world listing shows a description, genre, content count, and star rating.

## Star Ratings and Reviews

After playing in a community world, you can rate it from 1 to 5 stars. Ratings help other players discover quality content and give world creators valuable feedback on their work.

<Tip>Leave a rating after your first session. Even a quick star rating helps the community surface the best worlds.</Tip>

## Starting a Campaign in a Community World

Found a world you love? Starting a campaign is straightforward:

1. Open the world's detail page.
2. Click **Start Campaign** or **Create Campaign in This World**.
3. Configure your campaign settings (name, player count, difficulty).
4. Invite your friends or start a solo adventure.

The world's races, classes, items, spells, locations, and monsters are all available to your campaign from the start.

## Featured and Official Worlds

Some worlds are highlighted as **Featured** based on community ratings and editorial picks. Official worlds created by the LOREKEEPER team are also available and serve as great starting points for new players.

<Note>Community worlds are created by players like you. If you enjoy a world, consider building and sharing your own. Check out the World Building guides to get started.</Note>
