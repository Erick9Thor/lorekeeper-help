## Let Others Play in Your World

After building a world you are proud of, you can share it with the entire LOREKEEPER community. Other players can browse, rate, and start campaigns in your creation.

## Publishing Your World

To publish your world:

1. Open your world from the **My Worlds** section.
2. Review your content to make sure races, classes, items, and locations are complete.
3. Click **Publish to Community** from the world settings.
4. Your world becomes visible in the Community Worlds browser.

You can unpublish at any time if you want to make changes privately before sharing again.

## Star Ratings and Feedback

Once published, players who use your world can rate it from 1 to 5 stars. High-rated worlds appear more prominently in search results and may be selected as Featured worlds by the editorial team.

<Tip>A compelling world description and well-defined starter content (at least a few races, classes, and locations) tend to earn the highest ratings.</Tip>

## World Visibility

You control whether your world is **private** or **public**:

- **Private** -- Only you can use this world in your campaigns.
- **Public** -- Published to the community for anyone to discover and play in.

## Translating Your World

LOREKEEPER supports four languages: Spanish, English, Catalan, and Portuguese. You can translate your world's content to make it accessible to a wider audience.

- Translation is available from the World Editor.
- Each entity (race, class, item, spell, location, monster) costs **1 credit** to translate.
- Translations are generated by AI and can be reviewed and edited by you.

<Note>Translating your world dramatically increases its reach. A world available in all four languages can be discovered by the entire LOREKEEPER player base.</Note>
