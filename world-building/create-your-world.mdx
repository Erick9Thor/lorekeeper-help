## Build the World You Want to Play In

LOREKEEPER lets you create fully custom worlds with their own races, classes, items, spells, locations, and monsters. Your imagination sets the limits.

## Requirements

World creation requires a **paid plan** with an available editable world slot. The number of world slots depends on your subscription tier. Check your plan details in account settings to see how many slots you have.

## The World Creation Wizard

Creating a world starts with a guided wizard:

1. **Name** -- Give your world a memorable name.
2. **Genre** -- Choose from 11 available genres (Classic Fantasy, Cyberpunk, Space Opera, and more).
3. **Description** -- Write a summary that captures the tone and setting of your world.

Once the basics are set, you land in the World Editor where the real building begins.

## Adding Content

From the World Editor, you can create and manage:

- **Races** -- Define species with unique attributes and lore.
- **Classes** -- Design character archetypes with distinct abilities.
- **Items** -- Craft weapons, armor, potions, and artifacts.
- **Spells** -- Build magical abilities with effects and costs.
- **Locations** -- Map out cities, dungeons, wilderness, and landmarks.
- **Monsters** -- Populate your world with creatures and enemies.

<Tip>Start with a few core races and classes, then expand as your campaigns reveal what your world needs. You do not have to build everything at once.</Tip>

## World Builder Bot

Every world comes with access to the **World Builder Bot**, an AI assistant that helps you generate content. Describe what you want and the bot creates drafts for races, items, locations, and more. Free plans include 20 messages; paid plans get additional capacity.

<Note>The World Builder Bot generates suggestions that you can edit and refine. You always have final say over what goes into your world.</Note>
