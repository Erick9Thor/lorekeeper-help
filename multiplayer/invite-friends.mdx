## Sharing Your Adventure

Playing LOREKEEPER with friends is one of the best ways to experience a tabletop RPG. Every campaign supports up to 6 players on all plans, including the free tier.

## How to Invite Players

There are two ways to bring friends into your campaign:

### Share Link

From your campaign dashboard, click the **Invite Players** button. This generates a unique link you can send via chat, email, or social media. Anyone with the link can request to join.

### Invite Code

Each campaign has a short invite code displayed on the campaign page. Share this code with your friends and they can enter it from their dashboard to find and join your game.

## What Your Friends Need

Your friends only need a **free LOREKEEPER account** to join. They do not need a paid subscription. Once they create an account and accept the invitation, they can build their character and jump into the story.

<Tip>Friends can sign up and join in under two minutes. Send them the link and they will be ready before the next session.</Tip>

## Who Pays for the Game?

The campaign owner funds the adventure. Your daily turns are consumed when any player in the campaign takes actions. This means your friends play for free while you manage the turn budget.

<Note>Plan your sessions around your available daily turns. Upgrading your plan gives the whole group more turns to work with.</Note>

## Managing Your Party

As the campaign owner, you can view pending invitations, accept or decline join requests, and remove players from the campaign settings page. You also have full control over campaign configuration and pacing.
